How to play

The keys to move are W, A, S, and D. W is the up for the mover, s is down, a is left and d is right.

Arrow keys do not work, the WASD pair of keys after all is more user friendly and simple to understand.

Rules

The number one rule of the Maze Game Deluxe is simple do not hit the green walls.

You are also not allowed to exit the maze unless you reach the yellow finish square.

Reward

If you complete all levels then you are listed on the Winners list.
